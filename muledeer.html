Mule Deer

Species Overview

Mule deer, order Artiodactyla; Family Cervidae; Genus Odocoileus; Secies O. heminus.
Mule deer are an on the move species, taking their herd to higher ground during the summer months to enjoy cooler temperatures and better feed and spending the winter months on the low ground.
Common feed types are grasses, oak brush, bitter brush, and will even make special efforts to get into farmer/residential crops if available.
