Bighorn Sheep

Species Overview

Order Artiodactyle; Family Bovidae; Genus Ovis; Species O. canadensis (Rocky Mountain/ Desert Sub-species).
Sheep herds will often find a place in high barren cliffs to dwell permanently. Roaming only when food/water runs out.

Hunter's Perspective

Trophy Qualities

Hunting is almost exclusively for male sheep known as "Rams". Trophy qualities include horn mass and curl length.

Food Source Value

Food source is considered a delicacy. Not a ton of meet is secured off of a mature ram, but what meat can be taken is usually valued highley among hunters and family.

Difficulty & Permit Access

Probably the most exclusive hunt in the United States. Harvesting a sheep is considered a "once-in-a-lifetime" opportunity in most states.

Predation & Population Management

Predators & Threats

Everything is an enemy to sheep. High transfer of disease and small slow bodies make them a very sensative species. It's often wondered how they made it this far without going extinct. In many places without human intervention sheep would no longer exist.

Population Regulation Needs

Population needs to be regulated to slow the spread of disease. Hunting also raises money to keep sheep alive through water an habitat projects.

Hunting Justification

Not enough sheep permits are issued to consider hunting a population managment tactic. Instead hunting a sheep is so expensive that water an habitat are built from money that is paid to hunt a sheep.
